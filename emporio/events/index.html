Brand Race

May the odds be ever in your favour

In this event, teams will be required to create their own product and a company.

Teams will also be required to devise various marketing strategies to sell their product in the market.

Each team will get two hours to create their product, promote it and sell it.

Teams will have to provide:

  • Name of the company
  • Logo of the company
  • Estimated production cost
  • MRP of the product

Quiz

Brain it on

The quiz will comprise of two rounds.

In the first round, a questionnaire will be sent to all the schools via email which are to be answered by the contesting participants from each school.

This questionnaire will consist of objective as well as subjective questions.

The top 6 teams scoring the highest in the first round will be invited for the 2nd round.

Questions will be related to important events in the Indian and global history related to economics, business and accountancy.

The final round will consist of 5 stages:

  • Companies and logos
  • Identification
  • Benchmark events in the world of economics
  • Rapid fire

Brochure Making

When it's too hot to think

In this event, the participant will be required to prepare a brochure of any given type of company.

The company will be allotted on a random basis by the means of chits.

Participant will be required to carry his own art material but will be given the sheet in the school itself. Participant will be free to use the sheet in any way- cut, fold or color it.

For example: topics may be tours and travel company, real estate company etc.

Each participant will be given one and a half hour.
